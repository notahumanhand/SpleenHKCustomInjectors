# SpleenHKCustomInjectors

This is a collection of all of the custom injectors I have made for use with the CustomPoolInjector and CustomConstraintInjector connections for Hollow Knight's Randomizer 4 mod. These have been playtested and are for the most part functional, though there have historically been some hash-related issues when playing with ItemSync for one or two of these.

## Custom Constraint Injectors

**FrenchVanillaClaw**: Attempts to place Mantis Claw somewhere in Mantis Village.
**Grub Palace**: Places as many grubs as possible in White Palace.
**Sleepover**: Places as many dreamers as possible in White Palace.

### Preplaced

**BadTimeAtColo**: Places a grub at each colo reward, and places scarecrows at various colo enemies. 
**BasinSimpleClaw**: Places Mantis Claw at Basin Simple Key.
**CorniferParty**: Places a scarecrow at every map location.
**NothingStag**: Places a "Nothing??" (lumafly escape) at every stag location. Has a side effect of double-charging for stags. It is possible to charge more geo than a player actually has, resulting in negative geo.
**Scamifer**: Places a "Nothing??" (lumafly escape) at every map location. Has a side effect of double-charging for stags. It is possible to charge more geo than a player actually has, resulting in negative geo.
**SpiritsLibrary**: Places a Lore Tablet Kingdom's Edge at every ghost essence location in glade. Does not prevent other items from being placed there.
**Sploded**: Places a jelly egg bomb at various inconvenient enemy journal entry locations.
**VanillaClaw**: Places Mantis Claw at Mantis Claw.
**VanillaSealOfBinding**: Places Seal of Binding at Seal of Binding.
**VoidClaw**: Places Mantis Claw at the Birthplace Void Heart location.
